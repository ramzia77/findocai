COMMERCIAL LOAN AGREEMENT

This Commercial Loan Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of March 3, 2025,
by and between Meridian Community Bank, N.A. ("Lender"), and Harborview
Logistics LLC ("Borrower"). Borrower's authorized signatory, Dana Whitfield,
may be reached for account matters; Borrower's tax ID on file is 47-1928374
and the disbursement account number is 00293841057.

SECTION 1. THE LOAN

1.1 Principal Amount. Subject to the terms of this Agreement, Lender agrees to
lend to Borrower the principal sum of $850,000.00 (the "Loan").

1.2 Interest Rate. The Loan shall bear interest at a fixed rate of 6.75% per
annum, calculated on the basis of a 360-day year.

1.3 Maturity Date. The outstanding principal and all accrued interest shall be
due and payable in full on March 3, 2032 (the "Maturity Date").

SECTION 2. PAYMENT TERMS

2.1 Monthly Installments. Borrower shall repay the Loan in 84 consecutive
monthly installments of principal and interest, beginning April 1, 2025.

2.2 Prepayment. Borrower may prepay the Loan in whole or in part at any time
without penalty, provided Lender receives ten (10) days prior written notice.

SECTION 3. COVENANTS

3.1 Affirmative Covenants. Borrower shall maintain in full force and effect
its legal existence, comply with all applicable laws, and furnish Lender
with annual audited financial statements within 120 days of fiscal year end.

3.2 Negative Covenants. Without Lender's prior written consent, Borrower
shall not (a) incur additional indebtedness in excess of $100,000, (b) merge
or consolidate with any other entity, or (c) sell substantially all of its
assets.

3.3 Financial Covenants. Borrower shall maintain a minimum debt service
coverage ratio of 1.25:1.00, tested quarterly.

SECTION 4. EVENTS OF DEFAULT

4.1 Default. Each of the following constitutes an "Event of Default" under
this Agreement: (a) failure to pay any installment within ten (10) days of
its due date; (b) breach of any covenant in Section 3; (c) Borrower's
insolvency or filing for bankruptcy protection.

4.2 Remedies. Upon an Event of Default, Lender may declare the entire unpaid
principal balance and accrued interest immediately due and payable.

SECTION 5. GOVERNING LAW

5.1 This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the
laws of the State of Illinois, without regard to its conflict of laws
principles.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the date
first written above.

Meridian Community Bank, N.A. (Lender)
Harborview Logistics LLC (Borrower)
