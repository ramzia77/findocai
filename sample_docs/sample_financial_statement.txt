ITEM 7. MANAGEMENT'S DISCUSSION AND ANALYSIS

Overview. Northfield Industrial Holdings, Inc. ("the Company") reported total
revenue of $482.3 million for fiscal year 2024, an increase of 8.4% compared
to $444.9 million in fiscal year 2023. The increase was primarily driven by
growth in the Company's industrial components segment.

Results of Operations. Net income for fiscal year 2024 was $37.6 million, or
$1.92 per diluted share, compared to $31.1 million, or $1.58 per diluted
share, in fiscal year 2023. Operating margin improved to 11.2% from 10.4% in
the prior year, reflecting cost discipline and pricing improvements.

Liquidity and Capital Resources. As of December 31, 2024, the Company held
$64.2 million in cash and cash equivalents and had $120.0 million available
under its revolving credit facility. Total long-term debt was $210.5 million,
resulting in a net debt to EBITDA ratio of 1.9x.
